## Buckets: Track Your Income & Expenses 💰

**Buckets** is a mobile application designed to help you manage your personal finances with ease. With Buckets, you can track your income and expenses, create budgets, and monitor your financial goals. The app aims to give you a clear picture of where your money is going, enabling you to make smarter financial decisions.

This app is built with Flutter, enabling cross-platform development.

## Main Features ✨

*   **Easy Transaction Logging ✍️**: Quickly and easily record your income and expenses, with options to add categories and notes.

*   **Data Visualization 📊**: Gain insights into your spending habits with clear and informative charts and graphs.

*   **Customizable Categories 🗂️**: Categorize your transactions based on your needs, allowing for more detailed expense analysis.

*   **Budgeting 🎯**: Set budgets for different categories and track your progress toward achieving your financial goals.
